01 — Start Here
→ Two schools, a pre-application first +

Element and Elite both start with a pre-application before the full application gets sent. Elite's first step is quick — but Element's is more involved, with real detail on your background and your classes wanted up front. So know which one you're walking into before you start.

At a minimum, both ask for the basics first:

— Contact info
— A description of your services + a category
— Delivery format (in person / virtual)

Heavier items (W-9, Certificate of Insurance, proof of business) come at the full stage — all from your standard packet.
⚠ Use your own info. Element is open for applications now — and since its pre-application is the in-depth one, gather your details before you start.
