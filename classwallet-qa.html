Vendor Training Reference
01 — Access & Roles
Q If I want to add a VA but don't want them to see or change my financial info, should I add them as an Admin or a User? What if I did want them to be able to do invoicing for me? +

For a VA who should not touch your financial info, add them as a User. For someone handling invoicing, you'd move up to Admin.

User Role

✓View Payment Reports
✓Manage their own login credentials
✗Cannot see or change bank account
✗Cannot invite other users
✗Cannot edit vendor profile

Admin Role

Everything in User, plus:

✓Update linked bank account
✓Invite other users & admins
✓Manage vendor profile & display name

02 — Search & Discoverability
Q In my display name, are the keywords I'm using searchable by families looking for my classes? +

Yes — but families must search in a specific place.

Families need to use the Direct Pay section to find you. Under that search bar, they can type your full display name (e.g., "1 Art – Megapixels School") or even just the number "1" and your school will appear in results.

The general search elsewhere in the platform may not surface your name — Direct Pay is the key entry point.

03 — Credentials
Q Does it make sense to upload a college degree in Credentials even if it's not a teaching degree? Who sees these, and what are they used for? +

No need to upload it — at least not right now.

The credentials section is currently for your own personal visibility only. It is not linked to your application and is not visible to families, so uploading a degree (teaching or otherwise) won't affect how you appear in the system.

This may change as the platform evolves, but for now you can skip this section entirely.
04 — Vendor Categories
Q Can I be listed in more than one vendor category? Would multiple categories make me show up more often in search? +

Yes, you can apply for multiple categories. However, only one primary category will display on your actual profile.

This doesn't limit your visibility. As long as you're approved for additional services, families can still find and select you whenever they search for those specific categories in the system.
05 — Payments & Invoicing
Q For Payment Reports, is that where I'd go to do the invoicing? +

The Payment Report is your dashboard for viewing payments — not where invoicing originates. Transactions are initiated manually by parents, not by you.

Here's how the Direct Pay flow works:

1. You send an invoice to the parent.

2. The parent manually submits the payment through their ClassWallet account.

3. Once approved by the program administrator, you receive a deposit in 2–10 business days.

4. The transaction then appears in your Payment Report.

Note: payments pending administrator approval will not yet be visible in the report.

06 — Profile
Q How long can my vendor description be? +

There's no official character limit. The description box is generous — many vendors write very detailed, lengthy descriptions without any issues.

Feel free to include everything families need to know. Don't hold back!
07 — Platform Setup
Q For ClassWallet, once a vendor sets up their account, does it work for all schools — or do they have to set it up separately for each school? +

For ClassWallet, you only have to set up your account once, and it will already work for all the schools.

For Odyssey, it's different — each ESA under Odyssey has its own portal link, so vendors will need to set up their account separately for each school.

For both ClassWallet and Odyssey, the information a vendor puts in does show up in the vendor directory.
