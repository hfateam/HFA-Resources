Raven Homeschool runs through the Yukon-Koyukuk School District in Alaska, so its rules look pretty different from the California charters. The big thing to understand is how you get paid — they offer two payment tracks.

04 — Keep in Mind
→ The takeaways +

Two things to remember: choose between the Purchase Order and Family Payment tracks, and treat Alaska's requirements as their own thing, separate from everything California. Have your syllabus and fee schedule ready and you're in good shape.
