01 — Start Here
→ A quick base, with a few extras for Arete +

Epic California is one of the more streamlined applications — a nice quick win. Arete Charter Academy is the same idea with a stack of extra compliance items. So do Epic as your base, and if you're applying to Arete too, add its extras at the end.

⚠ Use your own info throughout. Most of the base comes straight from your standard packet, and several of Arete's extras are explained in plain English in the Common Extras reference.
02 — The Base
→ What the lightweight form asks for +
— Contact info + description of services
— W-9
— Certificate of Insurance ($1M minimum)
— Signed vendor / independent contractor agreement
— DOJ background check / Live Scan
— Custodian of Records (COR) option
— Accuracy / truth certification (signature)

Nearly all of this is in your standard packet already.

05 — Keep in Mind
→ Before you submit +

Epic is your quick, clean one. Arete is the same foundation with a compliance layer — and the nice part is that several of those items (TB clearance, Additional Insured Endorsement) come up at other schools too, so once you've done them you can reuse them.

⚠ The Additional Insured Endorsement takes a few days from your insurer — request it early so it doesn't stall your Arete application.
