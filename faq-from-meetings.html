Odyssey & Vendor FAQ

Quick answers to the questions that come up most often around logins, uploads, training, and insurance.

Is there a different login for each Odyssey school?+

Yes. Each school has its own separate Odyssey portal with its own login, and you'll see the state or school name right in the URL.

  • Wyoming ESA: wyoming-vendors.withodyssey.com/login
  • Louisiana GATOR: lagator-vendors.withodyssey.com/login
  • Utah Fits All: ufa-vendors.withodyssey.com/login

These links also live inside this lesson and in the School Directory.

I keep getting an "invalid username or password" error on Odyssey. What should I do?+

First, double-check you're using the correct Odyssey link for the school you're trying to reach. Each school has a different URL, and the wrong link is the most common cause of login errors.

Next, make sure you're logged out of every other Odyssey account before logging into a different one.

If it's still not working, try a fresh Chrome window or clear your cache, then reach out to Odyssey Support.

I'm getting an error when uploading my CSV to Odyssey. What's wrong?+

Check that your category matches exactly what the portal has listed. The category text has to be an exact match to the portal's existing options, right down to the singular vs. plural.

For example, Online Class (singular) may throw an error, while Online Classes (plural) is the correct category.

What specific Mandated Reporter Trainings do vendors need to complete?+

If you're completing the training through the California Department of Social Services (CDSS) portal, you'll need both of these:

  • School Personnel | CA
    Approx. 3.5 hours · certificate costs $8.99
  • Child Abuse Prevention Training for School Personnel (SB 848)
    Approx. 36 minutes · certificate is free
