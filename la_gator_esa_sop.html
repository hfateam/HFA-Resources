01 — About This Program
→ What is the LA GATOR ESA, and why should I apply? +

The Louisiana GATOR Scholarship Program is Louisiana's first education savings account (ESA), managed through the Odyssey platform. As an approved vendor or service provider, families can use their ESA funds to pay for your educational services directly through the Odyssey Marketplace.

All vendors and offerings are reviewed by both Odyssey and the Louisiana Department of Education (LDOE) before approval. The application is straightforward — just make sure you have your documents ready before you start.

02 — Eligibility
→ Do I qualify to apply? +

To participate in the LA GATOR Scholarship Program Marketplace, you must meet these requirements:

✓
Operate as a US entity
✓
Offer services or products eligible under the program's guidelines
✓
Schools must provide a core curriculum (math, English, science) and tests required for a state-weighted diploma
Hover over the eligible education expense categories on the dashboard before starting your application to confirm your services qualify.
03 — Provider Types
→ Which provider type should I select? +

When completing your application, you'll select one provider type from a dropdown. Hover over each option to read the full definition before choosing — the form updates based on your selection and shows required documents specific to your type.

Full-Time Student-Facing
Responsible for a student's full-time education. Directly interacts with students while delivering services. Examples: microschools, online schools (not BESE-approved).
Part-Time Student-Facing
Offers educational goods or services on a part-time basis with direct student interaction. Examples: tutors, therapists, public schools offering individual courses or special ed services.
Vendor
Offers educational goods or services without direct student interaction. Examples: curriculum providers, educational materials suppliers.
⚠ Once you choose a provider type, the form updates to show required documents. Choose carefully — picking the right category determines what you'll need to submit.
04 — Before You Start
→ What do I need to have ready before I begin? +

Have everything below ready before you start — the form must be completed in one session or saved using Save & Exit.

✓
Business license
✓
LLC documentation / attestations
✓
Business address (as registered)
✓
Vendor description — what you offer, who you serve, what makes your services valuable
✓
Hours of operation
✓
Primary contact details (name, title, phone — Odyssey use only)
⚠ Documents required may vary by provider type — the form will tell you exactly what's needed once you select yours.
05 — Application Steps
→ How do I complete the application? +

Step 1 — Create your Odyssey account

1
Go to lagator-vendors.withodyssey.com and click Sign Up
2
Fill out the registration form and click Sign Up again
3
You'll be redirected to your Odyssey Vendor Account dashboard

Step 2 — Check eligibility and start

4
Hover over the eligible education expense categories to confirm your services qualify
5
Click Start Application under Home

Step 3 — Contact details

6
Enter your first name, title, and phone number — this is only visible to Odyssey, not parents

Step 4 — Business information

7
Enter your business name
8
Select your provider type — hover each option before choosing
9
Enter your business address
10
Add your vendor description and hours of operation

Step 5 — Upload documents

11
Upload your business license and LLC documentation. Additional documents may be required based on your provider type — the form will show you what's needed.

Step 6 — Vendor acknowledgement

12
Check all acknowledgement boxes
13
Sign the vendor acknowledgement form
06 — Submit or Save
→ What are my options at the end of the form? +

At the final screen, you have two options:

Submit
Sends your completed application to Odyssey and LDOE for review. You're not locked in until you hit this.
Save & Exit
Saves your progress so you can return and finish later. Use this if you need to gather more documents.
07 — After You Submit
→ What happens after I submit? +

Once submitted, Odyssey and LDOE will review your application. Here's what to do next:

1
Watch for a confirmation or follow-up email from the Odyssey team
2
Once approved, set up your Stripe account to receive payments from families
3
List your offerings in the LA GATOR Marketplace so families can find and book you
Odyssey has separate guides for Stripe setup and listing your offerings. See the Masterlist for LA GATOR Vendors on the Odyssey Support Center for all post-approval resources.
